Welcome to Wild World!

It's a game based on Tower Defenders. Monsters come and you have to place towers to prevent them from reaching the end of the screen. Gold accumulates over time. Cannons cost 500 Gold.

Monsters move in a path and blow up when shot. See how many levels you can pass!!
